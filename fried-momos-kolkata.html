Fried Momos in Kolkata

Crispy golden Himalayan dumplings served hot with spicy chutney

Fried momos are a crispy and indulgent twist on the classic Himalayan dumpling. While steamed momos are loved for their lightness, fried momos take things up a notch by pan frying or deep frying the dumplings until they develop a golden, crispy outer layer. The contrast between the crunchy wrapper and the juicy filling inside makes fried momos an irresistible treat for street food lovers in Kolkata.

The art of making great fried momos lies in getting the wrapper just right. At Momo Heaven we first steam our momos and then fry them to create the perfect texture — crispy on the outside and soft and juicy on the inside. This two step process ensures that the filling stays moist while the wrapper develops a beautiful golden color and satisfying crunch.

At Momo Heaven we serve fried momos in both chicken and veg varieties. Our fried chicken momos are filled with fresh seasoned chicken and fried to golden perfection. Our fried veg momos offer the same satisfying crunch with a delicious vegetarian filling. Both varieties are served hot and fresh with our signature spicy chutney on the side.

Fried momos have become one of the most popular street food choices in Kolkata especially among young food lovers who enjoy the extra crunch and texture. If you are looking for a more indulgent momo experience then our fried momos are the perfect choice. Visit Momo Heaven and enjoy the crispiest Darjeeling style fried momos in Kolkata.
