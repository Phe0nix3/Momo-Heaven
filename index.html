Authentic Himalayan & Darjeeling Style Momos

Darjeeling is famous for its delicious Himalayan street-style momos, a beloved dumpling dish enjoyed across the mountain region. These soft dumplings are known for their thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and spicy red chutney. Inspired by this rich food culture, Momo Heaven brings the authentic taste of Darjeeling momos to Kolkata.

Our menu includes freshly prepared chicken and veg momos inspired by traditional Darjeeling recipes. Each plate captures the warm Himalayan flavors that have made Darjeeling momos popular among food lovers across India.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of momos does Momo Heaven serve?

    We serve steamed chicken momos, fried chicken momos, spicy chicken momos, steamed veg momos and fried veg momos. All momos are prepared fresh daily using traditional Darjeeling style recipes.

  • How much do momos cost at Momo Heaven?

    Our momos start at ₹70 for veg momos and go up to ₹120 for spicy chicken momos and fried chicken momos. Each plate comes with 6 pieces and spicy chutney.

  • Are steamed momos healthy?

    Yes! Steamed momos are one of the healthiest street food options in Kolkata. They are low in oil, high in protein, and made with fresh ingredients making them a great light meal or snack.

  • What makes Darjeeling style momos different?

    Darjeeling style momos are known for their thin handmade wrappers, juicy fillings, and bold spicy chutney. They are inspired by the famous street food culture of the Himalayan hill town of Darjeeling.

  • Do you serve vegetarian momos?

    Yes! We serve fresh vegetarian momos including steamed veg momos, fried veg momos and spicy veg momos. All our veg momos are filled with fresh vegetables and Himalayan spices.
