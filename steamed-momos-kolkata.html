Steamed Momos in Kolkata

Light and healthy Himalayan dumplings served hot with spicy chutney

Steamed momos are the most traditional and healthy form of Himalayan dumplings. Originally from the mountain regions of Darjeeling and Tibet, steamed momos are prepared by placing fresh dumplings over boiling water and letting the steam cook them slowly. The result is a soft, delicate dumpling that is light on the stomach and full of natural flavor.

What makes steamed momos so popular in Kolkata is their simplicity. Unlike fried momos, steamed momos preserve the natural taste of the filling without any added oil. Every bite delivers the pure flavor of the seasoned filling wrapped in a thin, soft dough. They are the perfect choice for anyone looking for a lighter and healthier street food option.

At Momo Heaven we prepare fresh steamed momos every day using traditional Himalayan recipes. Our steamed momos are available in both chicken and veg varieties, each filled with fresh ingredients and seasoned with ginger, garlic, and aromatic spices. Every plate is served hot with our signature spicy chutney inspired by Darjeeling street food.

Steamed momos have become one of the most popular snacks in Kolkata among health conscious food lovers. They are low in oil, high in protein, and incredibly satisfying. Whether you are enjoying them as a quick snack or a light meal, steamed momos from Momo Heaven are always a great choice.
