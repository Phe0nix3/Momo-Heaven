Best Darjeeling Momos in Kolkata

Darjeeling is famous for its delicious Himalayan-style momos, a street food loved by locals and travelers alike. These soft dumplings are known for their thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and spicy red chutney served on the side.

At Momo Heaven, we bring the authentic taste of Darjeeling to Kolkata with freshly prepared chicken and veg momos inspired by traditional Himalayan recipes.

Each plate captures the flavors of Darjeeling street food culture while offering momo lovers in Kolkata a fresh and satisfying dining experience.
