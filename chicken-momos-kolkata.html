Chicken Momos in Kolkata

Chicken momos are one of the most popular street foods in Kolkata. Inspired by the famous dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these soft and flavorful dumplings are loved for their juicy fillings and spicy chutney.

At Momo Heaven we prepare fresh chicken momos every day using simple ingredients and traditional Himalayan recipes. Our menu includes steamed chicken momos, crispy fried chicken momos, and spicy chicken momos for those who enjoy bold flavors.

Steamed Chicken Momos

Steamed chicken momos are the most traditional style of Himalayan dumplings. They are made with thin wrappers filled with seasoned chicken and gently steamed until soft and juicy.

This classic preparation keeps the dumplings light and flavorful and is usually served with spicy tomato and chili chutney inspired by Darjeeling street food.

Fried Chicken Momos

Fried chicken momos offer a crispy twist on the traditional dumpling. After steaming, the dumplings are lightly fried to create a golden outer layer while keeping the juicy chicken filling inside.

This version is popular among street food lovers who enjoy the combination of crunchy texture and rich savory flavor.

Spicy Chicken Momos

Spicy chicken momos are perfect for those who enjoy bold flavors. These dumplings are tossed in a flavorful spicy sauce made with chili, garlic, and aromatic spices.

The spicy coating enhances the taste of the dumplings and creates a delicious street food experience inspired by Himalayan cuisine.
