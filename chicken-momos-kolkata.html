Juicy Chicken Momos in Kolkata

Chicken momos are one of the most popular street foods in Kolkata. These soft dumplings are filled with flavorful minced chicken, fresh herbs, and spices that create a juicy and satisfying bite.

Inspired by the famous street stalls of Darjeeling, Momo Heaven brings authentic Himalayan-style chicken momos to Kolkata. Each dumpling is carefully wrapped with a thin dough layer and filled with seasoned chicken to deliver rich and comforting flavors.

Our chicken momos are served hot with spicy red chutney that perfectly complements the juicy dumplings. Whether steamed or fried, these Himalayan dumplings are a favorite among momo lovers across the city.
