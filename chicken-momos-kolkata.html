Chicken Momos in Kolkata

Fresh Darjeeling style Himalayan dumplings served hot with spicy chutney

Chicken momos are one of the most loved street foods in Kolkata. Inspired by the famous dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these soft and flavorful dumplings are known for their thin wrappers and juicy chicken fillings.

At Momo Heaven we prepare fresh chicken momos every day using traditional Himalayan recipes. Our menu includes steamed chicken momos, crispy fried chicken momos, and spicy chicken momos served with flavorful chutney.

Steamed Chicken Momos

Traditional Himalayan dumplings filled with seasoned chicken and gently steamed until soft and juicy.

Fried Chicken Momos

Crispy chicken dumplings lightly fried to create a golden outer layer with juicy filling inside.

Spicy Chicken Momos

Chicken dumplings tossed in a bold spicy sauce made with chili, garlic and aromatic spices.
