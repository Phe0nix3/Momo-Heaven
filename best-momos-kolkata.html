Best Momos in Kolkata

Fresh Darjeeling style Himalayan dumplings served hot with spicy chutney

Kolkata has a vibrant street food culture and momos have become one of the most popular snacks across the city. Inspired by the famous street stalls of Darjeeling, these Himalayan dumplings are loved for their thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and flavorful chutney. From busy street corners to college canteens, momos are now one of the most searched street foods in Kolkata.

The secret behind great momos lies in the freshness of ingredients and the care taken during preparation. A good momo has a thin, smooth wrapper that holds together when picked up but breaks open softly in the mouth. The filling should be juicy, well seasoned, and full of flavor. At Momo Heaven we follow these principles with every single plate we serve.
