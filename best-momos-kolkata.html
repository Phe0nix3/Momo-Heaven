Best Momos in Kolkata

Kolkata is known for its vibrant street food culture, and momos have become one of the most loved snacks in the city. Originally popular in the Himalayan regions like Darjeeling, these soft dumplings have found a special place in Kolkata’s food scene. Today, momo lovers across the city enjoy steamed, fried, and spicy versions of this delicious dish.

At Momo Heaven, we bring the authentic taste of Darjeeling-style Himalayan momos to Kolkata. Inspired by the street stalls of Darjeeling, our dumplings are prepared fresh with thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and traditional spices. Each plate is served hot with flavorful chutney that perfectly complements the dumplings.

Our menu includes a variety of options for momo lovers. From steamed chicken momos to crispy fried momos and fresh vegetarian dumplings, every plate is made with care to deliver the taste people expect when searching for the best momos in Kolkata.

Momos are more than just a snack — they are a comfort food for many people in the city. Whether enjoyed with friends in the evening or as a quick bite during a busy day, these Himalayan dumplings continue to win hearts across Kolkata.
