Best Momos in Kolkata

Kolkata has a vibrant street food culture, and momos have become one of the most popular snacks enjoyed across the city. Inspired by the famous street stalls of Darjeeling, these Himalayan-style dumplings are loved for their thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and flavorful chutney.

At Momo Heaven, we bring the authentic taste of Himalayan dumplings to Kolkata with freshly prepared momos made every day. Our menu includes steamed chicken momos, crispy fried momos, and fresh vegetarian dumplings.

Each plate is served hot with spicy chutney that enhances the rich flavors of the dumplings. Whether you prefer chicken momos or vegetarian options, our recipes are inspired by traditional Darjeeling street food culture.
