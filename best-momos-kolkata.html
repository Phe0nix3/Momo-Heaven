Why Momos Are So Popular in Kolkata

Over the last decade, momos have become one of the most loved street foods in Kolkata. From busy markets to small food stalls, these Himalayan dumplings are enjoyed by people of all ages. Their soft wrappers, juicy fillings, and spicy chutney make them a perfect quick snack.

Originally popular in Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, momos have now become a staple street food across the city. Food lovers searching for the best momos in Kolkata often look for authentic Darjeeling-style recipes that capture the true taste of Himalayan cuisine.
