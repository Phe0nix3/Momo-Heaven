Darjeeling Style Momos in Kolkata

Darjeeling is famous for its delicious Himalayan-style momos, a street food loved by locals and travelers alike. These soft dumplings are known for their thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and spicy chutney served on the side.

Inspired by the street stalls of Darjeeling, Momo Heaven brings the authentic taste of Darjeeling-style momos to Kolkata. Our recipes are influenced by traditional Himalayan dumplings that have become popular across the region.

At Momo Heaven, we prepare fresh momos every day, offering both chicken and vegetarian dumplings served hot with flavorful chutney. These Himalayan dumplings capture the rich taste and street food culture of Darjeeling.
