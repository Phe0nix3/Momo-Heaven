Darjeeling Style Momos in Kolkata

Authentic Himalayan dumplings inspired by Darjeeling street food

Darjeeling style momos are famous across India for their soft wrappers, juicy fillings, and spicy chutney. Originally popular in the hill town of Darjeeling, these Himalayan dumplings have become one of the most loved street foods in Kolkata. The cool mountain climate of Darjeeling gave birth to this warming street food that locals and tourists have enjoyed for generations.

What makes Darjeeling style momos unique is the way they are prepared. The dough is rolled thin by hand, filled with fresh ingredients, and folded into a half moon shape before being steamed over boiling water. The result is a soft, delicate dumpling that bursts with flavor in every bite. The spicy red chutney served alongside is made from fresh chilies and gives the momos their bold, fiery kick.

At Momo Heaven we bring the authentic taste of Darjeeling street food to Kolkata with freshly prepared Himalayan dumplings made using traditional recipes and simple ingredients. Whether you prefer steamed or fried, chicken or veg, every plate we serve captures the true spirit of Darjeeling momo culture.

Kolkata has always embraced the food culture of the hills, and momos have found a special place in the hearts of food lovers across the city. From college canteens to street stalls, momos are now one of the most searched street foods in Kolkata. At Momo Heaven we take pride in serving momos that stay true to their Himalayan roots.
