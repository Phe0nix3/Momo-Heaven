Darjeeling Style Momos in Kolkata

Darjeeling style momos are famous across India for their thin wrappers, juicy fillings, and spicy Himalayan chutney. Originally popular in the hill town of Darjeeling, these dumplings have become one of the most loved street foods in Kolkata.

At Momo Heaven we bring the authentic taste of Darjeeling street food to Kolkata with freshly prepared Himalayan dumplings inspired by traditional recipes.

What Makes Darjeeling Momos Special

Darjeeling momos are known for their delicate wrappers and flavorful fillings. They are usually served steamed and accompanied by a spicy chutney made from chili, garlic, and tomatoes.

Authentic Himalayan Dumplings

Our menu includes chicken momos, veg momos, and fried dumplings prepared fresh every day using simple ingredients and traditional Himalayan cooking techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Darjeeling style momos?

They are Himalayan dumplings with thin wrappers and juicy fillings.
