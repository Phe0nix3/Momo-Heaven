Spicy Momos in Kolkata

Fiery Himalayan dumplings tossed in bold chili sauce with garlic and spices

Spicy momos are one of the most exciting variations of Himalayan dumplings. Born from the bold flavors of Darjeeling street food, spicy momos take the classic steamed or fried dumpling and toss it in a fiery chili sauce made with fresh chilies, garlic, and aromatic spices. The result is a bold, intense flavor that keeps spice lovers coming back for more.

What makes spicy momos unique is the chili sauce. At Momo Heaven our spicy sauce is prepared fresh using a blend of red chilies, garlic, ginger, and Himalayan spices. The sauce is bold and fiery but balanced enough to let the flavor of the dumpling shine through. Every bite delivers a perfect combination of heat and flavor that is truly addictive.

At Momo Heaven we serve spicy momos in both chicken and veg varieties. Our spicy chicken momos are filled with fresh seasoned chicken and tossed in our signature chili sauce. Our spicy veg momos offer the same bold flavor with a delicious vegetarian filling. Both varieties are served hot and fresh with extra chutney on the side.

Spicy momos have become extremely popular among young food lovers in Kolkata who love bold and fiery flavors. If you are someone who enjoys a good kick of spice with your street food then our spicy momos are the perfect choice for you. Visit Momo Heaven and experience the fiery side of Darjeeling style Himalayan dumplings right here in Kolkata.
