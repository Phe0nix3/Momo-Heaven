🥬 Why Veg Momos Are a Kolkata Favorite

Veg momos are one of the most popular vegetarian street foods in Kolkata. Inspired by the famous dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these dumplings are loved for their thin wrappers and flavorful vegetable fillings. They are a perfect snack for vegetarians who want to enjoy authentic Himalayan street food.

What makes veg momos so special is the freshness of the filling. At Momo Heaven we use a mix of fresh cabbage, carrots, onions, and capsicum seasoned with ginger, garlic, and aromatic Himalayan spices. The filling is prepared daily to make sure every dumpling is packed with flavor and nutrition.

Whether you are a vegetarian or simply looking for a lighter option, our veg momos offer the perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Come experience the best vegetarian Himalayan dumplings in Kolkata!
