Veg Momos in Kolkata

Fresh vegetarian Himalayan dumplings served hot with spicy chutney

Veg momos are a popular vegetarian street food in Kolkata. Inspired by the famous dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these dumplings are loved for their thin wrappers and flavorful vegetable fillings.

At Momo Heaven we prepare fresh vegetarian momos every day using traditional Himalayan recipes. Our menu includes steamed veg momos, crispy fried veg momos and spicy veg momos served with delicious chutney.

Steamed Veg Momos

Traditional vegetarian dumplings filled with cabbage, carrots and Himalayan spices.

Fried Veg Momos

Crispy vegetarian dumplings with golden wrappers and flavorful vegetable filling.

Spicy Veg Momos

Veg dumplings tossed in spicy chili sauce with garlic and aromatic spices.
