Veg Momos in Kolkata

Fresh vegetarian Himalayan dumplings served hot with spicy chutney

Veg momos are a popular vegetarian street food in Kolkata. Inspired by the famous dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these dumplings are loved for their thin wrappers and flavorful vegetable fillings. They are a perfect snack for vegetarians who want to enjoy the authentic taste of Himalayan street food.

What makes veg momos so special is the freshness of the filling. At Momo Heaven we use a mix of fresh vegetables seasoned with ginger, garlic, and aromatic spices. The filling is prepared daily to make sure every dumpling is packed with flavor and nutrition.

At Momo Heaven we prepare fresh vegetarian momos every day using traditional Himalayan recipes. Our menu includes steamed veg momos, crispy fried veg momos and spicy veg momos served with delicious chutney. Each variety has its own unique taste and texture that keeps our customers coming back for more.

Veg momos have become one of the most popular snacks in Kolkata among students, families, and food lovers alike. Whether you are vegetarian or simply looking for a lighter option, our veg momos offer the perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Come visit Momo Heaven and experience the best vegetarian Himalayan dumplings in Kolkata.
