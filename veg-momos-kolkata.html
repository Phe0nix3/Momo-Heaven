🌱 The Best Veg Momos in Kolkata

Veg momos are one of the most beloved vegetarian street foods in Kolkata. Inspired by the famous dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these dumplings are loved for their thin wrappers and flavorful vegetable fillings. At Momo Heaven, we take pride in serving the freshest vegetarian dumplings made with quality ingredients.

What makes our veg momos special? We use a fresh mix of cabbage, carrots, onions, and capsicum, seasoned with ginger, garlic, and aromatic Himalayan spices. Every dumpling is made fresh when you order — never frozen or pre-steamed.

  • 🍳 Pan-Fried Veg Momo — ₹100 (6 pcs) — Crispy golden bottom, soft steamed top — the perfect texture
