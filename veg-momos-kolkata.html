Fresh Veg Momos in Kolkata

Vegetarian momos have become one of the most loved street foods in Kolkata. These soft dumplings are filled with a flavorful mix of fresh vegetables, herbs, and spices that create a light yet delicious dish enjoyed by many momo lovers.

Inspired by the famous street-style momos of Darjeeling, Momo Heaven brings authentic Himalayan vegetarian dumplings to Kolkata. Each momo is carefully prepared using a thin dough wrapper and filled with fresh vegetables to create a perfectly balanced flavor.

Our veg momos are served hot with spicy chutney that complements the delicate taste of the dumplings. Whether steamed or fried, these vegetarian momos offer a satisfying and comforting snack for anyone looking for fresh veg momos in Kolkata.
