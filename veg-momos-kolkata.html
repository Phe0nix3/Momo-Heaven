Veg Momos in Kolkata

Veg momos are a popular vegetarian street food in Kolkata. Inspired by the traditional dumplings of Darjeeling and the Himalayan region, these dumplings are known for their soft wrappers and flavorful vegetable fillings.

At Momo Heaven we prepare fresh vegetarian momos every day using simple ingredients and traditional Himalayan recipes. Our veg dumplings are served hot with spicy chutney inspired by Darjeeling street food.

Steamed Veg Momos

Steamed veg momos are the classic style of vegetarian Himalayan dumplings. They are made with thin wrappers filled with fresh vegetables like cabbage, carrots, and aromatic spices.

The dumplings are gently steamed until soft and juicy, making them a light and flavorful snack that pairs perfectly with spicy chili chutney.

Fried Veg Momos

Fried veg momos offer a crispy twist on traditional vegetarian dumplings. After steaming, the dumplings are lightly fried to create a golden outer layer while keeping the vegetable filling soft inside.

This version is especially popular among street food lovers who enjoy a crunchy texture combined with the rich flavors of Himalayan cuisine.
